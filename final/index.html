Hello! My name is Preet Kulkarni.
I'm a third year Computer Science and Engineering student at Manipal University Jaipur.

As a CSE student, I am passionate about developing my technical skills and utilizing emerging technology to make a positive impact on the world as a whole. I have proficiency in languages such as C++, Python and Java and am currently expanding my skill set by learning full-stack web development. I also have experience in working with various database tools such as MongoDB and SQL, which contributes towards my ability to build comprehensive and dynamic web applications.

Besides my technical skills, I have gained valuable experience in leadership and teamwork through participation in various projects and extracurricular activities. I have organized and managed teams for hackathons, cultural events, and social initiatives, which has helped me develop adaptability, strong communication and organizational skills.

My ultimate goal is to leverage my technical expertise, leadership capabilities, and creativity to contribute to impactful projects that make a difference.
